Find Your College/University


What kind of college are you looking for?

Are you looking for a 2 or 4 year college/university? At a two-year college/university, students can earn an associate degree while at a four-year college/university, students can earn a bachelor's degree

Are you looking at whether a college/university is public or private? Many students exclude private colleges due to their expensive costs, but with financial aid the cost can be reduced to the cost of a public college/university.

Is location a problem for you?

For some students location may have a big impact on which college they’ll choose. Some students aren’t willing to leave too far away from home, meanwhile others are willing to go far away. A good amount of students like to go home on weekends while others will go home during their vacation.

If you are looking forward to go away from home, are you thinking of getting a dorm or look for an apartment out of campus? Do you want a greek life in college? Are you looking for a Rural, Suburban or Urban Environment?

What are you looking forward in the learning Environment?

Do you care about the amount of people on campus? Do you care about the number of students in a class? Some people like an academic challenging environment while others like an academically comfortable environment. Are you looking forward to seeing a variety of different type of people?

Does the college/university you are looking at have the activities you like?

Whatever college you choose to spend the next 2-4 years of life, make sure it has the activities you are interested in. Don’t forget to check out the wide variety of clubs the college has. It could be from your favorite sport to that hobby of yours that you only do at home.

Does the college/university have the major you like? This is the most important part of choosing your college/university. Are you interested in extended class availability?

Is tuition a restriction for you?

Do you have a preference in tuition? Is there a percentage of Financial Aid that you need? Are there any Work-Study programs offered? Does the college/university have a college application waiver fee. Are you planning to go to an international college? If so does your college/university offer international financial aid ?
